Downloads

This page allows you to download different datasets used by DarkJargon.net. Verified and user-created dark term datasets are created as up-to-date database dumps in CSV format.
Please consider citing our paper if you make use of our datasets. Thank you!

Verified Dark Terms A list of expert-verified dark terms as found on the home page. Download
User Dark Terms Dark terms that where contributed by users and are unverified. Download
